# Descripción del Puesto

**Cargo:** Senior Data Analyst
**Empresa:** World Wide Technology
**Fecha de aplicación:** 2025-11-12

## Descripción



The Senior Data Analyst in the Marketing department will lead end-to-end delivery of data requests, ensuring alignment with marketing goals and strategies. This entails engaging with stakeholders to deeply understand their business needs and how data requests contribute to our marketing strategies. The successful candidate will be responsible for analyzing complex data sets, building data sources, and influencing data-driven decisions related to digital and physical marketing efforts, UX design, and business impact. The role extends beyond report generation; the analyst will actively contribute to the advancement of our team's innovation and creativity. This includes proactively researching insights, and recommending projects and experiments aligned with marketing goals.
