# Descripción del Puesto

**Cargo:** Business Intelligence Analyst
**Empresa:** Insight Global
**Fecha de aplicación:** 2025-11-10

## Descripción


The role involves developing new reporting solutions tailored to meet evolving business requirements. It includes performing break-fix and enhancement activities on existing reporting systems to ensure continued functionality and improvement. A key responsibility is conducting thorough data validation to maintain the accuracy and reliability of reports. The position requires close collaboration with cross-functional teams to understand and address diverse reporting needs. Additionally, the role entails monitoring system performance and troubleshooting issues as they arise. Documenting processes, solutions, and enhancements is essential for future reference and knowledge sharing. Staying current with the latest trends and best practices in Tableau and Salesforce reporting is also a vital part of the job.
