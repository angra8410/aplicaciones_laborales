# Descripción del Puesto

**Cargo:** Power BI Developer
**Empresa:** Kaeus
**Fecha de aplicación:** 2025-11-12

## Descripción


The role involves working closely with stakeholders to design, develop, and deploy Power BI dashboards and visual reports. Responsibilities include performing data modeling, integrating data sources, and optimizing data flow. The Power BI Developer will also collaborate with teams to maintain data governance and meet analytics-related business requirements.
